Research Interests

My research focuses on understanding the requirements of emerging communication paradigms and leveraging their intrinsic properties to design, optimize, and develop next-generation communication protocols. I am an active contributor to the IEEE P3388 working group, where I support the standardization of performance assessment and measurement methodologies for industrial wireless systems. In addition to my core research interests, I have expanded my work to support aspiring Indian researchers. I mentor and train students to meet international research standards, helping them secure fully funded PhD admissions at leading universities across the world.
